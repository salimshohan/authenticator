TIB 2FA Vault

Version 1.0.0 (Pro)

A highly secure, zero-knowledge authenticator app designed to keep your digital life safe and accessible from anywhere.

How We Protect You

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Your 2FA keys are encrypted locally on your device using AES-256 before being sent to the cloud. We cannot see or access your codes.

Master PIN System

Your Master PIN is never stored on our servers. It acts as the ultimate decryption key that only you possess.

Cloud Synchronization

Powered by Supabase, your encrypted vault is securely synced in real-time, meaning you never lose your codes if you lose your device.

Offline Generation

Once your vault is unlocked, TOTP codes are generated entirely offline in your browser for maximum speed and security.
