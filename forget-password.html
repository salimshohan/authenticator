Password Recovery Guide

Please read the following information carefully regarding your vault security.

Secure Recovery Link

The reset link is securely generated and will expire in a short period. Do not share this link with anyone.

Master PIN Warning

Resetting your account password does NOT reset your Master PIN. If you have lost your Master PIN, your data cannot be decrypted.

Check Spam Folder

If you don't see the email in your inbox within a few minutes, please check your spam or junk folder.
