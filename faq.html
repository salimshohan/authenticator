Help & FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the Vault.

What is a Master PIN?
Your Master PIN is a secret code that only you know. It acts as a digital key. Before your 2FA codes are uploaded to the cloud, the app uses your PIN to lock (encrypt) them. When you log in, it uses the PIN to unlock them.
What happens if I forget my Master PIN?
Because we use Zero-Knowledge Encryption, your Master PIN is never sent to our servers. If you lose it, it is mathematically impossible to recover your data. You will have to clear your vault and start over. Please memorize your PIN!
Is my data safe from hackers?
Yes. All your 2FA secrets are encrypted locally on your device using AES Encryption before they ever touch the internet. Even if our database is compromised, the hackers will only see scrambled text. They cannot read your codes without your Master PIN.
How do I add a new authenticator?
1. Go to the security settings of the service you want to secure (e.g., GitHub, Facebook).
2. Choose to setup an Authenticator App.
3. Look for the option that says "Can't scan QR code" or "Enter manual code".
4. Copy the long Base32 Secret Key provided and paste it into this app.
Can I access my codes offline?
You need an internet connection to log into the app and fetch your encrypted data. However, once the vault is unlocked on your screen, the 6-digit TOTP codes are generated entirely offline in your browser.
