Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our highest priority.

Last Updated: May 2026

TL;DR (Too Long; Didn't Read)

We use Zero-Knowledge Encryption. This means your 2FA codes are encrypted on your device using your Master PIN before being saved to our cloud. We cannot see, read, or sell your 2FA codes. You are the only one who holds the key to your data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our secure synchronization services, we collect the following minimal information:

  • Account Information: Your email address and an encrypted password used exclusively for account authentication.
  • Profile Data: Any optional metadata you choose to provide in your profile (e.g., Name, Phone).
  • Vault Data: Your 2FA Base32 secrets, which are strictly encrypted locally on your device prior to transmission.

2. How Your Data is Secured

We implement a strict Zero-Knowledge architecture:

  • Local Encryption: Your 2FA secrets are encrypted using the Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) within your web browser.
  • Master PIN: Your Master PIN is the encryption key. It is never transmitted to, nor stored on, our servers.
  • Offline Generation: Time-Based One-Time Passwords (TOTP) are calculated locally in your browser environment.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We utilize trusted third-party infrastructure to operate the app:

  • Supabase: Used as our secure backend-as-a-service (BaaS) for authentication and database hosting. Supabase complies with strict industry security standards.
  • GitHub Pages: Used to securely host the frontend static files of this application.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to save your application preferences (e.g., Dark Mode settings, Auto-lock timers) and to temporarily cache your session state to keep you logged in securely.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual 2FA codes or wipe your entire vault from the App Settings at any time. When you choose to delete your data, it is permanently removed from our active database.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us via the Contact Page.
